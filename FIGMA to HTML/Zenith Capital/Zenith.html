What’s Real Estate Syndication?

It’s simple: real estate syndication is when a bunch of investors pool their money to buy bigger, better properties—stuff you couldn’t buy on your own. Most investors (limited partners) just put in their cash and relax, while the experts (general partners) handle the day-to-day and share the profits.

In the U.S., this is a tried-and-true way to invest in commercial real estate.
